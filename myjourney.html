Journey

MY

JOURNEY

Year 1: Adjusting and dealing with homesickness is very important. It's completely normal to feel homesick, but use this time to start building new relationships with friends and explore new things in university life. Year 2: Your plan to fully focus on your studies is excellent. Once you’ve adjusted, you’ll be able to enjoy life more and manage your time better. Year 3: Starting to seek more life experiences, such as internships or joining various activities, will be very beneficial in preparing for the working world. Year 4: By this year, you’ll be ready to step into the working life and begin pursuing your dreams.
